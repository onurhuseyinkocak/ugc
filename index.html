About

You will not need to teach me what your product does.

I build software and explain AI products for a living. I can turn a feature list into a useful story without flattening the product or overpromising.

Most UGC stops at "nice clip." I start from the business question - who's the buyer, what's the objection, what makes them stop scrolling - and design the content backwards from there. Hooks, pacing, on-screen copy and calls to action are all deliberate, because attention without conversion is just noise.

I create in fluent English and Turkish, so a single brief can reach a global audience and a Turkish-speaking one without losing tone or nuance. From Bangkok I work with founders and teams across every time zone - async, fast, and easy to brief.

  • Fast & remote - Bangkok-based, working with brands worldwide.

FAQ

Questions brands ask.

The real questions founders and marketers type into Google - or ask ChatGPT - when they're looking for a creator like me.

What kind of brands do you make content for?
AI products, SaaS, mobile apps and technical startups. If the product needs explanation before it earns attention, it is a strong fit.
Do you create UGC in both English and Turkish?
Yes. I create in English and Turkish. The public samples here are Turkish, so ask for a short English test read with your brief.
Can the videos be used as paid ads?
Yes. Everything is built hook-first for TikTok, Reels and Shorts and works as organic content or paid creative. Usage rights, whitelisting and raw files are agreed up front.
Do you actually understand technical products?
I build software myself, so I can read a feature list and turn it into a story a buyer cares about - without getting the product wrong or over-promising.
How fast can you deliver?
Fast and fully remote from Bangkok. Most single videos land in days, not weeks; larger batches are scheduled around your launch date.
How does pricing work?
Scope-based - from a single test video to a monthly content package. Tell me the product, the goal and how many videos you need, and I'll send a clear quote.
Can you help with an app or product launch?
Yes - launch content is a core use case: explainer reels, product demos, founder videos and ad creatives, planned around your release.
Do you work with international brands?
Yes. I'm based in Bangkok and work with founders and teams worldwide - async and across time zones, in English or Turkish.
Do you cover lifestyle or travel content too?
Through my creator network: Mine Koçak for lifestyle & beauty, and Movia Travel Hub for travel & hospitality. One studio, three audiences.
Do you offer Canvas UGC and ongoing brand-account content?
Yes. We can plan, script, film and edit recurring short-form for a brand-owned or dedicated ambassador account, with optional posting and a repeatable testing calendar. The model is available across every brand category.
