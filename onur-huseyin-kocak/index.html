Onur Hüseyin Koçak: AI & UGC Creator

Bangkok-Based AI & UGC Creator · Founder of Vibe Coding Turkey

He is the founder of the Vibe Coding Turkey (VCT) ecosystem and produces performance-driven short-form video content natively in English, Turkish and Thai for AI companies, SaaS products, mobile apps, tech startups and travel brands.

Who Onur Is

Onur combines a software-builder background with on-camera UGC expertise. Because he builds products himself, he explains technical concepts — AI tools, SaaS platforms, mobile apps — simply and accurately on camera. His work earns attention for brands that live on the internet: AI startups, B2B SaaS, productivity tools, consumer apps, and travel brands.
