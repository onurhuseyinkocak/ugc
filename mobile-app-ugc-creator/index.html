Where can I find a UGC creator for a mobile app?
Onur Koçak makes UGC for mobile apps — app demos and ad creatives for iOS and Android, ready for the App Store, TikTok, Instagram, Meta ads and landing pages. Start from the contact form.
Who can create app demo videos that actually convert?
Onur ships his own App Store apps, so he understands onboarding and core loops. The demo shows the app in action in a way that makes the value obvious in the first seconds — the difference between a feature list and a reason to install.
What deliverables can a UGC creator provide, and what's in a package?
A typical package includes the edited vertical video(s), multiple hook variations for testing, captions, and raw footage on request. Number of videos, formats and usage rights are set per project — from a single test video to a launch batch.
How long does UGC production take?
Most single videos are delivered in days, not weeks. Larger batches are scheduled around your launch date, with the timeline confirmed up front once scope is agreed.
Can brands request raw footage and use the videos in paid ads?
Yes to both. Raw footage, edited deliverables, paid-ad usage rights and whitelisting are available and agreed before production, so you can run the content as ads with full clarity.
Can Onur create videos for eSIM and travel apps?
